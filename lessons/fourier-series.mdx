# Fourier Series

Any periodic function can be decomposed into a sum of sine and cosine waves.

f(t) = (4/π) · [ sin(t) + sin(3t)/3 + sin(5t)/5 + ... ]

This is the Fourier series — the mathematical foundation of signal processing, audio compression, and much more.
