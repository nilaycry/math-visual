# Gradient Descent

Gradient descent finds the minimum of a function by iteratively stepping in the direction of steepest descent.

x_{n+1} = x_n - α · ∇f(x_n)

The learning rate α controls the step size. Too large and you overshoot; too small and convergence is painfully slow.
